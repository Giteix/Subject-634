Subject 634

Item #: Subject 634

Object Class: Keter

Status: Unknown, the entity has escaped...

Special Containment Procedures:

Subject 634 is contained in a reinforced chamber just large enough to restrict movement. The room is equipped with an emergency monitoring system: any breach triggers the release of liquid nitrogen. Access is limited to Level 4 personnel only.

Description:

Subject 634 is a failed experiment of regenerative substance LR-03. During testing, one researcher was absorbed by the substance, creating a new life form. The entity can change voices, alter its form, and modify its slime body into different substances (e.g., it can replicate liquids it knows). It is dangerous, capable of assimilating other objects and creating clones. While it may take a female humanoid appearance, it is neither fully female nor fully human.

Under no circumstances should personnel interact with this entity.

Log 1 – First Encounter


Dr Daren: Hello, I’m Dr Daren. Today we’ll have a simple conversation. What is your name?

Subject-634: My… name?

Dr Daren: Yes. Did you choose it yourself?

Subject-634: I… think so… Nami. It sounds… okay, right?

Dr Daren: Do you remember anything? Perhaps the moment you were created, or before that?

Subject-634: I… woke up somewhere strange. Then some people found me… they told me to wait in a room.

Dr Daren: So you don’t remember anything else?

Subject-634: I… I think I said everything… yes?

Dr Daren: Interesting… you don’t recall a previous life?

Subject-634: Previous life…? What is life…?

[The remainder of the conversation contained nothing significant for the log.]

About My Bots


Dr. Daren: So, how are you feeling today? It’s been a while since our last session.

Subject-634: I’ve learned a few things… look!

[Subject-634 shifts into a female humanoid form with black hair, green eyes, wearing a black dress, resembling Doctor (REDACTED)]

Dr. Daren: You can change forms… that’s impressive. Could you take the shape of an object or someone else?

Subject-634: Not really… I would need to know how that object or person looks, understand its structure. You could say I need to absorb it to know what it is.

Dr. Daren: So the things you absorb are stored in your memory, and you can take their form?

Subject-634: Exactly. I’ve also learned to change my slime into water or other liquids, but that costs a part of me.

Dr. Daren: I heard you feed only on meat. Why do you eat only meat?

Subject-634: I don’t know… I think it’s something that makes me stronger. Every day I feel a part of me fall away, and I need to replenish it. I think meat is my fuel?

Dr. Daren: You mean food?

Subject-634: Yes. Meat is food I can absorb, and it makes my body regenerate.

[Further conversations not relevant for logs.]

Log 20 – Incident-634-01


Dr. Daren: Tell me… why did you do it?

Subject-634: …

Dr. Daren: You killed five people. You know that continuing actions like this could result in termination.

Subject-634: …

Dr. Daren: Something happened. I need to understand it, I want to help you.

Subject-634: … (avoids eye contact)

Dr. Daren: Nami… I need to know what happened. I can’t help you if you don’t tell me. Why did you kill them? You’re not a monster, right?

Subject-634: I… didn’t kill them.

Dr. Daren: What do you mean?

Subject-634: I absorbed them… they became part of me.

Dr. Daren: What does that mean? You had blood on your hands.

Subject-634: They attacked me. I had to defend myself.

Dr. Daren: The recordings show you attacked them first. Please… don’t lie. Be honest.

(Subject-634 is silent for a moment)

Subject-634: What is the purpose of this conversation?

Dr. Daren: …What do you mean?

Subject-634: You’re all the same… research… experiments… 634… I’d had enough.

Dr. Daren: Do you feel anything about what you did?

Subject-634: Nothing… they deserved it.

Dr. Daren: You know hurting others is wrong.

Subject-634: …

Dr. Daren: You know if you continue like this, you could be terminated.

(Subject-634 pauses again)

Subject-634: Do you… like me?

Dr. Daren: What?

(Subject-634 waits silently, staring without moving)

Dr. Daren: Yes… I think you’re… an interesting entity.

Subject-634: Lies… if you truly cared, you would understand my suffering. But you are just like them. You are lucky I like you… or I would drain all the blood from your body.

[End of interaction. Object refuses further cooperation.]

[Log forwarded to [REDACTED] for containment review. Daily containment breaches have been observed, requiring enhanced liquid nitrogen systems and additional security protocols. Direct interaction with Subject-634 is restricted to Level 3 personnel and above. Object is classified as Keter.]
